history of museum

The National Museum of Iran is a collection of historical and prehistoric treasures of Iran in Tehran, housed in two separate buildings: the Museum of Ancient Iran (opened in 1937) and the Museum of Archaeology and Art of the Islamic Era of Iran (opened in 1979) [1, 2]. It is considered the most important cultural center in the country and houses artifacts from the Paleolithic period to the Islamic era. The architecture of the Museum of Ancient Iran building was inspired by Sassanid architecture.
